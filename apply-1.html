  • 1. Visa Options

We provide "the Emergency Vietnam visa service" for those who would like to get visa right away, because some reasons out of your control. Before apply Express visa you must agree with our term and condition, and you suggest read and understand what is Express visa service and how it works. This problem usually happens to those who need to enter Vietnam in urgent but do not have time or forget to apply Vietnam visa in advance.

Some information below will help you better to understand how express for you to apply Vietnam visa services and how to choose on Emergency case.

  • Urgent Vietnam visa: This case for those who would like to get Vietnam visa in a few hours (4-8 working hours). From Monday to Friday. Not effective if you apply on Saturday and Sunday or Holiday and arrive Vietnam in the same day.
  • Emergency Vietnam visa: This service takes you maximum from 30 minutes to 4 hours. From Monday to Friday, even you apply Vietnam visa and arrive in the same day. But it will not effective if your visa application date and arrival date are same on Saturday, Sunday, and holiday.
  • Holiday Vietnam visa: For those who apply visa on Saturday, Sunday, or holiday and arrive Vietnam in the same day. However you will be charged more:
    • 190 USD/person for 1 month single entry visa
    • 190 USD/person for 3 months single entry visa
    • 215 USD/person for 1 month multiple entries visa
    • 215 USD/person for 3 months multiple entries visa
    for this case and we called " Penalty Vietnam visa service fee". And this is not including the stamping fee at the airport when you arrive. Please give us a call at: +84.909.343.525 or you can contact us for advice.

We suggest that you should apply visa 1 week before your arrival date, if not you will be in cases above.
