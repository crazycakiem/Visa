Visa fee payment and info

Cathy
United Kingdom
Visa fee payment and info
May 06, 2017
Hi
Myself and my partner are travelling to Vietnam in 10 days time for approximately 1 month, we will be applying for our visas online
Can you confirm if the costs to get our passport stamped at the airport (Ho Chi Minh) can be paid by credit card?
Also, do we need to have confirmed travel arrangements for exiting Vietnam?
Also, if we only stay in Vietnam for 2 weeks (which we may decide to do yet) do we need to apply online for a visa?
Thank you
Vietnam Evisa Support
May 08, 2017

Dear Ms Cathy,

Thank you for your questions.

Firstly, We would like to confirm you that according to the Vietnam Immigration's new policy, from July 1, 2015 to 31 Feb 2019, 12 countries (including France, Germany, Italy, Spain, UK and Belarus, Norway, Finland and Sweden, South korea, Japan, Denmark) no need Visa if they stay in Vietnam less than 15 days. However, the previous visit must be far at least 30 days the next visit and your passport's validity must be more than 6 months.

Secondly, kindly be informed that you do not have to show your return ticket at the airport about your exit date to the Immigration officer.

Also, please be noted that you have to pay for the stamping fee at the airport in cash (VND or USD), credit card is not accepted.

In case you want to stay longer, please apply Visa online at: https://www.vietnam-evisa.org/apply-visa.html

Best regards

Hanah
