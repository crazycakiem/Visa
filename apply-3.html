Passport Information

Tips:
  • Full name, date of birth, passport number and nationality must be exact and same as state on your passport.
  • Passport expiration date must has at least 6 months validity when arriving to Vietnam.
  • Upload passport data page image and photograph (straight looking without glasses). Only these image formats are allowed: *.jpg, *.png or *.gif and the maximum upload file size is 8MB.

6. VOCABULARY

- Date format: dd/mm/yyyy - 12th June 1980
- Time Zone: Vietnam standard time - GMT +7
- Working days: All week days except Saturday and Sunday. But we know how to get visa for you on Sat and Sun.
- Working hours: 24/7 everyday.
- Multiple / Single entry: Multiple is interpreted as multiple entries, not multiple applicants.
- Multiple entries means you are allowed to enter / exit Vietnam multiple times within a given period of time (1, 3 or 6 months). For example: 6 month multiple entry visa means you are allowed to enter / exit Vietnam several times from the intended date of arrival provided plus 6 calendar consecutive months.
